# Milestone 1 Readiness Checklist

- A normalized brief contract exists and is locked.
- Required env vars are documented in `ENV.example`.
- Prompt rules do not require fields absent from the brief contract.
- Theme rules and prompt rules do not contradict each other.
- The output bundle contract is documented and revision-scoped.
- Validation requires structural checks, theme readability checks, local asset resolution checks, and manual QA evidence.
- Optional deploy/QR helpers are not part of the required success path.
- Briefs, outputs, and uploaded assets are git-ignored.
- Remaining external dependencies are documented explicitly.
